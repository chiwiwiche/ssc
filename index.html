Logistics and Transportation

Transportation encompasses the physical movement of goods from one place to another. It involves various modes of transportation, each with its advantages and limitations:

Road Transport

This includes trucks, vans, and automobiles. It's flexible, widely accessible, and suitable for short to medium-distance.

Rail Transport

Trains are efficient for long-distance, bulk transportation of goods.

Maritime Transport

Ships carry the majority of global trade, especially for goods transported over long distances.

Air Transport

Airplanes provide the fastest mode of transportation, ideal for perishable or time-sensitive goods.
